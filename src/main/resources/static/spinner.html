CLASSIC THRILLER

Vertigo

1958 128 MIN star 8.3

A former police detective juggles wrestling with his personal demons and becoming obsessed with a hauntingly beautiful woman. This masterpiece of suspense explores themes of psychological manipulation, romantic obsession, and the fragile nature of identity against the breathtaking backdrop of mid-century San Francisco.
